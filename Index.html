PB&J Recipe

Learn how to make a simple but tasty peanut butter and jelly sandwich

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of any kind of bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly (Grape Jelly)

Instructions

  1. Lay the two slices of bread
  2. Spread peanut butter on one piece of bread
  3. Spead Jelly on the other piece of bread
  4. Put two slices together
  5. Enjoy!
